MR. ZEE CONSTRUCTIONS — UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS

1. Open your GitHub repository: mr-zee-constructions
2. Upload everything inside this folder, including the complete assets folder.
3. Choose "Commit changes".
4. Wait 2–5 minutes and refresh https://mrzeeconstructions.co.uk
5. On your phone, use an incognito/private tab if the old version is cached.

Important: Upload the assets folder as well, otherwise the project photographs will not display.
